# Glyph Mo

**Glyph Mo** is a comprehensive Glyph Matrix control application designed exclusively for the **Nothing Phone (4a) Pro**. It allows you to harness the full power of the 13x13 LED matrix on the back of your device through custom animations, music synchronization, and intelligent notification rules.

## Key Features

### Live Animation Control
- **Instant Playback:** Choose from a library of built-in animations like *Matrix Rain*, *Space Invader*, *Pacman*, and *Heartbeat*.
- **Live Preview:** See a real-time representation of the matrix directly in the app before it hits your hardware.
- **Import Custom Frames:** Bring in your own creations from the designer tool.

### Music Visualizer (Music Sync)
- **Audio Sensitivity:** Watch the matrix dance to your music using the built-in Android Visualizer.
- **Multiple Visual Styles:**
  - **Bars:** Classic frequency bars.
  - **Sphere:** Pulsating energy from the center.
  - **Ripple:** Waves spreading across the grid.
  - **Vortex:** Swirling light patterns.
- **Advanced Tuning:** Adjust gain and smoothing for the perfect visual response.

### Glyph Timer
- **Visual Countdown:** Keep track of time without looking at your screen.
- **Back-Side Display:** The matrix provides a clear, receding visual indicator of remaining time.

### Smart Glyph Rules
- **App-Specific Alerts:** Assign unique animations to different apps (e.g., WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack).
- **Customizable Parameters:** Set the specific **Duration** and **Brightness** (0-255) for each notification rule.
- **Background Service:** Works even when the app is closed, ensuring you never miss a custom alert.

## Hardware Requirements
- **Target Device:** Nothing Phone (4a) Pro
- **Hardware:** 25x25 LED Glyph Matrix
- **Compatibility:** This app will **not** work on other Nothing devices (Phone 1, 2, or 2a) due to specific matrix hardware requirements.

---

## Getting Started
1. **Enable Developer Options:** Ensure your Nothing Phone is ready for debugging.
2. **Grant Permissions:** The app requires *Notification Access* for Glyph Rules and *Audio Record* for Music Sync.
3. **Build:** Use Android Studio to build and install the APK.
